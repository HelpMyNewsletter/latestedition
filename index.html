Part 2

Your list has dead weight. Here's the 30-minute audit that will shock you.

Before you can compete with anyone, you need to know what you are actually working with. Most newsletter operators have NO IDEA how unhealthy their lists actually are.

Run these numbers RIGHT NOW

Pull your last 90 days of sends. Answer these 5 questions:

1. What % of your list has NEVER opened?
If it is over 20%, you have a problem. If it is over 40%, you have a crisis.

2. What % hasn't opened in 60+ days?
These are your zombies. They hurt deliverability and skew your metrics.

3. What's your actual engagement rate?
Opens + clicks divided by total sent. Anything under 25% means you are shouting into the void.

4. Where did your worst performers come from?
Check acquisition source. Low-quality sources = cut them in 2026.

5. Are you sending to unverified emails?
Hard bounces, spam traps, fake addresses-all killing your sender reputation.

What most of you will discover

30-50% of your list is dead weight. Subscribers who signed up 18 months ago, opened once, and ghosted. They are not coming back. And every send to them hurts your deliverability with Gmail and Outlook.

The hard truth

A clean list of 1,000 engaged subscribers will outperform a dirty list of 5,000 zombies. Every. Single. Time. Higher opens, higher clicks, better deliverability, more revenue.

Part 3

The cleanup protocol: What to do with zombies before January 1

You found your dead weight. Now what? Most operators panic and delete everyone who hasn't opened in 90 days. Wrong move.

The 3-phase cleanup (start December 26)

Phase 1: The Warning Shot (Dec 26-28)
Send ONE last-chance email to everyone who hasn't opened in 60+ days. Subject: "Are you still here?" or "Last chance to stay subscribed." Make them click to confirm interest. Track who responds.

Phase 2: The Purge (Dec 29-30)
Everyone who didn't click? Gone. Unsubscribe them. Yes, your total count will drop. Your engagement rate will SOAR. Your deliverability will improve overnight.

Phase 3: The Segmentation (Dec 31-Jan 1)
Tag everyone who DID respond. These are your MVPs. They get special content, early access, better offers. Reward engagement immediately.

Expected results

Your list might shrink 30-40%. Your open rate will jump 15-25 points. Your inbox placement will improve within 2 weeks. Your revenue per send will double.

What about the people who "might come back later"?

They won't. Data shows subscribers who go 90+ days without opening have less than 3% chance of ever engaging again. They are spam bait. Cut them loose.

Pro tip: Download the full list before you purge. If someone complains later (they won't), you have proof they were inactive. CYA always.
