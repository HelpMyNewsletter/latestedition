The Blueprint

4 steps to launch your paid community. In this order.

Skipping steps or getting the order wrong is the fastest way to launch a ghost town. Follow this sequence for sustainable growth and revenue.

01 — Validate

Confirm demand for a paid space. Before building, poll your audience. Ask what problems they need solved collectively, and what they'd pay to access. Look for signals of high engagement with existing content or discussion topics.

02 — Structure

Design your community's value. Define what members get, what's exclusive, and your pricing tiers. Consider a Monetization Path Finder to align with your unique content and audience.

03 — Platform

Choose the right home for engagement. This isn't just about features; it's about where your audience prefers to engage. Weigh integrated tools against standalone community platforms. And if Beehiiv is already your home base, keep an eye on the Daily Deep Dive into Beehiiv before you bolt on another platform.

04 — Launch

Roll out with a clear value proposition. Soft launch to your most engaged segment first. Gather feedback, iterate, and then scale your invitation to the wider newsletter audience, emphasizing the exclusive benefits and shared journey.
